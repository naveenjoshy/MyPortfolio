Who am I?

Highly resilient and proactive professional who bridges the gap between traditional operations and modern technology.

Driven by a lifelong passion for coding and an innate drive to optimize complex systems, I am a developer who bridges operational logic with scalable software. After earning my Computer Science degree, I spent 12 years driving efficiency in the cooperative banking sector. Rather than just managing operations, I used my technical background to independently engineer custom software that automated and streamlined core financial workflows. Today, I am channeling that decade of deep domain expertise, data security intuition, and leadership into modern web engineering. Currently mastering the MERN stack, I build robust, secure, and business-focused full-stack applications. For me, development is about turning operational bottlenecks into seamless digital experiences through clean code and reliable database architecture.
